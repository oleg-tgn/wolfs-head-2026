About

Wolf’s Head returns!

We continue a tradition that has already become a landmark event for the Georgian HEMA community. Once again, the tournament will bring together fencers ready to compete on the field in intense, fair competition.

Prepare for strong opponents and an atmosphere where every bout is a challenge. The tournament is organized by St. George's HEMA School.
